Weather is the state of the atmosphere at a particular time and place. It is influenced by various factors, including temperature, humidity, wind, precipitation, and cloud cover. Weather conditions can change rapidly, and they can vary greatly from one place to another.
Weather predictions are made by combining current weather data with numerical weather prediction (NWP). NWP models use mathematical equations to simulate the atmosphere and predict the future state of the weather. Weather data is collected from a variety of sources, including ground stations, weather balloons, satellites, and radar.
